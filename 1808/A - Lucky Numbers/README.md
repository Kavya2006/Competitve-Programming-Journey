And the number $$$111$$$ has all digits equal to $$$1$$$, so its luckiness is zero.</p><p>Hateehc is a famous Martian blogger who often flies to different corners of the solar system. To release interesting videos even faster, he decided to buy himself a starship. When he came to the store, he saw starships with numbers from $$$l$$$ to $$$r$$$ inclusively.
