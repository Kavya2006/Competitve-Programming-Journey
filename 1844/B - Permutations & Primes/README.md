<div class="header"><div class="title">B. For example, $$$2,5,13$$$ are prime numbers, but $$$1$$$ and $$$6$$$ are not prime numbers. </li><li> A permutation of $$$1,2,\dots,n$$$ is an array consisting of $$$n$$$ distinct integers from $$$1$$$ to $$$n$$$ in arbitrary order.
