</pre></div></div></div><div class="note"><div class="section-title">Note</div><p>In the first test case, an example of a grid in which no greedy path achieves the maximum value out of all down/right paths is: $$$$$$ \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 5 & 1 \\ 2 & 1 & 2 \\ 5 & 4 & 3 \\ \end{bmatrix} $$$$$$ Let $$$a_{i, j}$$$ denote the value of the cell in the $$$i$$$-th row and $$$j$$$-th column.
